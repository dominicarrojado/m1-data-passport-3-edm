Austria
There's definitely never a dull moment when you celebrate the New Year in Austria. All church bells ring at the stroke of midnight and the Austrian annual New Year tradition begins, which includes festive concert repertoires and magical fireworks displays.
Thailand
Begin your New Year by lighting up paper lanterns in Chiang Mai, or witness fireworks displays over a twinkling skyline at Bangkok's many rooftop bars. Though each Thai city is unique, they are all sure to bring New Year celebrations that are equally breathtaking.
Indonesia
If you want to usher in the New Year with some relaxation, Indonesia is the best place to go. Escape the busy city life and decompress at the country's gorgeous beaches, quiet mountainsides, or spa and yoga retreats.
Germany
Feast over delicious meals, get lucky charms and clink your champagne glasses just like the Germans do during New Year's Eve. When it comes to fun and memorable celebrations like this, Germany is definitely a destination you shouldn't miss.
South Korea
Discover Korea's East Sea where most people visit and witness the first sunrise of the year. Immerse yourself in Korea's mouthwatering cuisine and fascinating culture during the day and party the night away at Seoul's bustling and extravagant nightlife.
Spain
In Spain, the New Year celebrations start as early as December 31. The streets are filled with people and music, but if you're looking for a huge party, visit the famous Puerta del Sol Square in Madrid where it turns into a large festivity once the clock strikes twelve.
United Kingdom
During New Year's Eve, pubs and restaurants all over the UK gear up for parties that last until dawn. The next afternoon, more than 10,000 performers from all over the world will gather for a massive New Year's Day parade in London.
Australia
Gather with more than one million people over Sydney Harbour and watch the spectacular annual fireworks display. It's so extraordinary that is also anticipated by people from all over the world as well.
New Zealand
Experience the best outdoor celebration only at New Zealand Witness the splendour of nature as you ring in 2018 in some of the country's best camping festivals such as Rhythm & Vines, Northern Bass, Rhythm & Alps, and Highlife NYD Experience.
Philippines
In the Philippines, Filipinos go all out during New Year's Eve celebrations. From the endless array of food, firecrackers that come in various shapres and sizes, to interesting traditions, welcoming 2018 in the Pearl of the Orient Seas is definitely a must-try.
